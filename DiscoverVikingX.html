Who we are?

VIKINGX is not a generic bike brand.
From day one, the mission has been simple: build components designed for riders who push limits.

Our products are created for those who value progression, control, and confidence on the bike. Every frame and component is developed using proven geometries, engineered for durability, and validated through real-world riding, not just lab testing.

Rather than trying to be everything for everyone, VIKINGX focuses on what matters: delivering purpose-built equipment for Freeride and Dirty Jump riders who demand reliability when it counts.

What started in a California garage, surrounded by skate culture, noise, and the need to build something stronger, became the foundation of the brand. No offices. No investors. Just riders determined to create bikes that could actually handle the way they rode.


That DNA remains unchanged.

Today, VIKINGX has built a growing reputation across Latin America and Europe, supported not by massive advertising campaigns, but by real riders who trust the products every day.

Thousands of riders are already riding, jumping, and progressing with VikingX.

Because if you ride knowing you might crash and still go for the jump
this brand was built for you.
